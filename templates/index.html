How it works

This bot answers using only the project's knowledge_base. If the question is out of scope or unsupported, it will respond with a safe fallback.

Sample in-scope questions

  • What are your pricing models?
  • What is your SLA for Severity 1?
  • Can we sign an NDA?

Sample out-of-scope questions

  • What is Bitcoin price today?
  • Can you give me legal advice?

Notes

Confidence is computed from retrieval similarity. Low confidence triggers fallback. Sources show which KB files were used.
