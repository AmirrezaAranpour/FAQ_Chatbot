# Complete FAQ List (In-Scope)

This is the canonical in-scope FAQ set used by the chatbot (stable answers).

## Q1: What services do you offer?

We offer Discovery (initial assessment), MVP builds, data analytics & dashboards, internal automation/RAG chatbots, and maintenance & support.

**Sources:** services.md

## Q2: What is included in the Discovery session?

Discovery is a 60-minute session to understand goals and constraints. The deliverable is a 1–2 page summary plus a recommended solution approach.

**Sources:** services.md

## Q3: What do you deliver for an MVP build?

An MVP build includes a lightweight UI, core API, and an initial deployment. Deliverables are a demo link, source repository, and a runbook.

**Sources:** services.md

## Q4: Do you build data dashboards?

Yes—data analytics & dashboards includes connecting to your data sources and building decision-ready dashboards. Tools may include Metabase, Superset, or Power BI depending on your stack.

**Sources:** services.md

## Q5: Do you build internal RAG chatbots?

Yes—we can build an internal automation/RAG chatbot connected to your organization’s knowledge, with emphasis on grounded answers, clear boundaries, and response control.

**Sources:** services.md

## Q6: What does Maintenance & Support include?

Maintenance & Support covers bug fixes and small improvements under an agreed SLA, plus basic monitoring and issue triage during business hours.

**Sources:** services.md

## Q7: What are your pricing models?

We support Fixed Price (project-based) and Time & Materials (hourly/weekly).

**Sources:** pricing.md

## Q8: What are the payment terms for a Fixed Price project?

Typical Fixed Price milestones are 40% at start, 40% mid-project, and 20% on delivery.

**Sources:** pricing.md

## Q9: How does Time & Materials billing work?

Time & Materials is billed hourly/weekly and is best for evolving scope. Weekly time reports are provided.

**Sources:** pricing.md

## Q10: Is the Discovery session free?

The indicative ranges note that the Discovery session is free for the first session.

**Sources:** pricing.md

## Q11: Do you provide indicative price ranges?

Indicative (non-binding) ranges include: small MVP from €1,200+, data dashboard from €800+, and an internal RAG chatbot from €1,500+ (scope-dependent).

**Sources:** pricing.md

## Q12: What payment methods do you accept?

Payment methods listed are bank transfer or official invoice.

**Sources:** pricing.md

## Q13: When do you start work for Fixed Price projects?

For Fixed Price, work starts after the first milestone payment.

**Sources:** pricing.md

## Q14: What is your engagement process from start to finish?

The process is: (1) contact & needs assessment (NDA if needed), (2) proposal & timeline (scope, estimate, pricing model), (3) delivery in short sprints with regular check-ins, and (4) final delivery & handover.

**Sources:** process.md

## Q15: Do you deliver work in sprints?

Yes—delivery is done in short sprints, typically 1–2 weeks, with weekly check-ins and progress updates.

**Sources:** process.md

## Q16: Can we sign an NDA?

Yes—an NDA can be signed if needed during the initial contact and needs assessment stage.

**Sources:** process.md

## Q17: What happens after the first call?

After the initial contact & needs assessment, the next steps are: a proposal & timeline (scope, estimate, pricing model), then delivery in short 1–2 week iterations with regular check-ins.

**Sources:** process.md

## Q18: What is included in final delivery & handover?

Final delivery & handover includes delivering code, deployment notes, and brief training. Knowledge transfer (handover) is included.

**Sources:** process.md

## Q19: What are your support hours?

Support business hours are Monday to Friday, 09:00–17:00 (CET/CEST).

**Sources:** support.md

## Q20: What is your SLA for Severity 1?

For Severity 1 (critical outage), the initial response time is within 4 business hours.

**Sources:** support.md

## Q21: What are your SLA response times for Severity 2 and 3?

Severity 2 (major issue): initial response within 1 business day. Severity 3 (minor/request): initial response within 2 business days.

**Sources:** support.md

## Q22: What support channels do you offer?

Support channels include email support, and chat or ticketing if enabled for the client.

**Sources:** support.md

## Q23: Are new features included in SLA support?

The knowledge base notes that large product changes or new features are out of SLA and require separate estimation and approval.

**Sources:** support.md

## Q24: What is your privacy policy?

Client data is used only to deliver the project, and access is minimal on a need-to-know basis.

**Sources:** policies.md

## Q25: What is your refund policy for Fixed Price work?

If work has not started: full refund of the first milestone is possible. If work has started: refund is calculated based on completed work.

**Sources:** policies.md

## Q26: Can meetings be rescheduled?

Yes—meetings can be rescheduled with at least 24 hours notice.

**Sources:** policies.md

## Q27: What happens if we cancel a Fixed Price project?

Cancellation for Fixed Price follows the refund rules: if work has not started, a full refund of the first milestone is possible; if work has started, the refund is based on completed work.

**Sources:** policies.md

## Q28: Do you offer 24/7 support?

The knowledge base lists business-hours support (Mon–Fri, 09:00–17:00 CET/CEST). It also notes support can be extended by agreement, but 24/7 is not the default.

**Sources:** support.md

## Q29: Can you share an exact price list for all services?

The knowledge base provides indicative (non-binding) starting ranges for common projects, but it does not include an exact price list for every service. Exact pricing depends on scope and is defined in the proposal.

**Sources:** pricing.md, process.md

## Q30: Can we do Discovery first, then decide pricing later?

Yes—Discovery/needs assessment happens first, and the pricing model and estimates are agreed during the proposal & timeline stage.

**Sources:** process.md
