# Services

## 1) Discovery (Initial Assessment)
- 60-minute session to understand your goals and constraints
- Deliverable: a 1–2 page summary + recommended solution approach

## 2) MVP Build
- A usable first version of your product or internal tool
- Includes: lightweight UI, core API, and an initial deployment
- Deliverables: demo link, source repository, and runbook

## 3) Data Analytics & Dashboards
- Connect to your data sources and build decision-ready dashboards
- Tools may include: Metabase / Superset / Power BI (depending on your stack)

## 4) Internal Automation & RAG Chatbots
- Build an internal chatbot/automation connected to your organization’s knowledge
- Emphasis: grounded answers, clear boundaries, and response control

## 5) Maintenance & Support
- Bug fixes and small improvements under an agreed SLA
- Includes basic monitoring and issue triage during business hours
