# Pricing & Payments

## Pricing models
1) Fixed Price (project-based)
- Best for clearly defined scope (e.g., a small MVP)
- Typical milestones: 40% start, 40% mid, 20% delivery

2) Time & Materials (hourly/weekly)
- Best for evolving scope or uncertain requirements
- Weekly time reports are provided

## Indicative ranges (non-binding guidance)
- Discovery session: free for the first session
- Small MVP: from €1,200+ (scope-dependent)
- Data dashboard: from €800+
- RAG chatbot (internal): from €1,500+

## Payment methods
- Bank transfer or official invoice
- For Fixed Price, work starts after the first milestone payment
