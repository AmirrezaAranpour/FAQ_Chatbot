# Engagement Process

## Step 1: Contact & Needs Assessment
- Understand the problem and business goals
- NDA can be signed if needed

## Step 2: Proposal & Timeline
- Define scope, estimated timeline, and cost
- Agree on pricing model (Fixed Price / Time & Materials)

## Step 3: Delivery in short iterations
- Work in short sprints (typically 1–2 weeks)
- Weekly check-ins and progress updates

## Step 4: Final delivery & handover
- Deliver code + deployment notes + brief training
- Knowledge transfer (handover) is included


---
## After the first call (next steps)
After the initial contact & needs assessment, the next steps are:
- We prepare a proposal & timeline (scope, estimate, pricing model)
- Then we deliver in short iterations (typically 1–2 week sprints) with regular check-ins
